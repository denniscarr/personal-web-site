NERVE DAMAGE

VISUALLY EXCESSIVE FIRST PERSON ACTION
-- COMING SOON TO STEAM --



Nerve Damage combines first-person score attack gameplay with an experimental visual style. Designed to evoke sensations of panic, mental disarray, and dissociation, while still being fun, Nerve Damage is influenced as much by noise music and abstract art as it is by classic action games.

Nerve Damage is currently available in demo-form for the Looking Glass holographic display, and was given an Honorable Mention for the Nuovo award at the 2019 Independent Games Festival.


FEATURES:


  • Arcade-style score attack gameplay
  • Unique weapon-tuning system
  • Online leaderboards
  • Original soundtrack
  • Many levels

THEMES:


  • Dissociation
  • Panic
  • Anxiety
  • Overstimulation
  • Noise
  • Stress

INSPIRATIONS:


  • Tempest
  • Robotron 2084
  • Doom [original]
  • Steina and Woody Vasulka
  • Tapestry weaving
Nerve Damage is based on a prototype created for the 2017 Global Game Jam. Play the original version here!
