Professional Projects

Please Leave Me Alone, I Need to Poop

A workplace comedy visual novel/minigame collection developed in Unity and released on Steam, Google Play and the App Store.

I programmed all game systems, protoyped new minigames, implemented assets, scripted animations, rigged the UI, implemented ad support for mobile versions, added achievement integration for all three storefronts, and handled the build/release process for all platforms.

Highlandia

A mobile app designed for a charter school to help teach English as a second language to adults using gamified learning, and released on Google Play.

I worked with Create Neptune as a programmer and designer on a medium-sized team. In addition to adding new features fixed bugs throughout the codebase, I also did the following:

  • Designed, pitched and prototyped new minigames.
  • Helped develop a workflow for generating and importing AI voice samples using a spreadsheet-based content management system.
  • Worked with the faculty at our charter school client to write content for the curriculum.
  • Designed levels and implemented art assets.
  • Rigged and scripted UI.

QluQuest

QluQuest is a prototype infinite runner/quiz game developed for Android. I worked with a small team at Create Neptune as a programmer and designer. After taking over from a very early prototype, I worked with an artist to revamp the game with new art, and added an infinite runner minigame.

  • Designed, protoyped and an infinite runner minigame with randomly-generated levels.
  • Reskinned quiz sequences with final art and implemented new booster mechanics with unique animations.
  • Designed and programmed an interactive map screen with several animations.
  • Scripted animations, rigged menus, and implemented sound effects for all parts of the game.

Playable Ads

From 2019 to 2023, I worked in the ad tech space, designing and programming playable advertisements in Javascript. These ads took the form of bite-sized games and were designed to run on desktop and mobile.

I first worked as a programmer at a startup called TreSensa Technologies, where I was promoted to team lead. When TreSensa was accquired by Vungle and then merged with Liftoff Mobile, I retained my position as the senior member of the game programming team.

During my time at these companies I accomplished the following:

  • Programmed over 40 playable ads for game and non-game brands.
  • Programmed over 20 playable ad builders for our SaaS model where clients could build ads themselves in an intuitive web-based interface.
  • Programmed tools for our modulate game engine including a particle effect system, a 2D camera system, animated sprites, and more.
  • Wrote and maintained documentation for our internal game engine.
  • Held internal standups, helped build and manage timelines, and helped hire and onboard new team members.
  • Led an initiative to gather more detailed analytics from our games to help assess our work and build internal best-practices.
  • Held periodic internal gamejams to help develop new ideas to keep us competitive with other studios.
