Professional Work

Please Leave Me Alone, I Need to Poop

A workplace comedy visual novel and minigame collection developed in Unity and released on Steam, Google Play and the App Store.

I programmed all game systems, protoyped new minigames, implemented assets, scripted animations, rigged the UI, implemented ad support for mobile versions, added achievement integration for all three storefronts, and handled the build/release process for all platforms.

Highlandia

Mobile app built in Unity, designed to help teach English as a second language using gamified learning. Released on Google Play.

I worked with Create Neptune as a programmer and designer on a medium-sized team. In addition to adding new features and fixing bugs throughout the codebase, I also did the following:

  • Designed, pitched and prototyped new minigames.
  • Helped develop a workflow for generating and importing AI voice samples using a spreadsheet-based content management system.
  • Designed 3D enviroments and implemented art assets.
  • Rigged UI.
  • Worked with the faculty at our charter school client to develop game content matching their curriculum.

QluQuest

QluQuest is an infinite runner quiz game developed in Unity for mobile devices. I worked with a small team at Create Neptune as a programmer and designer. I worked with an artist to revamp a rough prototype with new art, and added an infinite runner minigame.

  • Designed and programmed an infinite runner minigame with randomly-generated levels.
  • Reskinned quiz sequences with final art and implemented new booster mechanics with unique animations.
  • Designed and programmed an interactive map screen.
  • Scripted animations, rigged UI, and implemented sound effects for all parts of the game.

Playable Ads

From 2019 to 2023, I worked in ad tech, designing and programming playable advertisements in Javascript. These ads took the form of bite-sized games and were designed to run in browsers on desktop and mobile.

I first worked as a programmer at a startup called TreSensa Technologies, where I was promoted to team lead. When TreSensa was accquired by Vungle and then merged with Liftoff Mobile, I retained my position as senior member of the game programming team.

During my time at these companies I accomplished the following:

  • Programmed over 40 playable ads for game and non-game brands.
  • Programmed over 20 playable ad builders for our SaaS model where clients could build ads themselves in an intuitive web-based interface.
  • Programmed modules for our game engine including a particle effect system, a 2D camera system, animated sprite support, and more.
  • Wrote and maintained documentation for our internal game engine.
  • Held meetings, helped build and manage timelines, and helped hire and onboard new team members.
  • Led an initiative to gather detailed analytics from our games to help assess our work and build internal best-practices.
  • Held periodic internal gamejams to help develop new ideas to keep us competitive with other studios.
