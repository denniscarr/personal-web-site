About Me

ihavefivehat, aka Dennis Carr is a game programmer and designer living in New York City.

He is an expert at making games in engines like Unity, Unreal and Godot, and in web frameworks like Phaser and React. In his client-based work, he has published games for desktop, mobile and web, on platforms like Steam, Google Play and iOS.

His personal work has been nominated for awards at the Independent Games Festival, and has been featured in publications like Gamasutra, Kill Screen, The AV Club, and IGN.

Dennis is currently working on Nerve Damage and Smush Bois.
